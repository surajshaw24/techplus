About TechPlus Academy

Director: Suraj Shaw

At TechPlus Academy, we are dedicated to providing high-quality computer education that empowers individuals to succeed in today’s digital world. Located in Chirkunda, Dhanbad, Jharkhand (828202), we take pride in being a trusted name in technology training for students, professionals, and career seekers. Our mission is simple — to make learning comfortable, accessible, and effective. We believe that clear concepts and practical skills are the foundation of success. That’s why every course we offer is designed with a hands-on, real-world approach, ensuring our students are fully prepared to meet real-world challenges. Join us today and take the first step toward a successful future with TechPlus Academy! .
