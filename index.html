About Us

Your Journey to Professional Beauty Expertise

Welcome to Sunidhi Makeover & Academy, Jharkhand's premier destination for professional beauty education. Founded by award-winning makeup artist Sunidhi Shaw, our academy combines creativity, technical skill, and industry knowledge to transform passionate individuals into beauty professionals.

With over 5 years of experience in bridal, fashion, and editorial makeup, Sunidhi brings real-world expertise to every class. Our hands-on training approach ensures you graduate with the confidence and skills to excel in the competitive beauty industry.

Our state-of-the-art training facility in Chirkunda, Dhanbad features professional-grade products, equipment, and lighting to simulate real salon environments. We maintain small class sizes for personalized attention and maximum practice time.

Beyond technical skills, we focus on client communication, business development, and staying ahead of industry trends to prepare you for a successful career.
