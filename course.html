Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior experience to enroll?

Most of our beginner and intermediate courses require no prior experience, though basic computer literacy is recommended. Our advanced courses may have prerequisites which are clearly listed in the course description.

What's the time commitment for these courses?

Course durations vary from 3-8 months depending on the program. Most students spend 10-15 hours per week on coursework, including live sessions, self-study, and projects.

Are the courses conducted online or in-person?

All our courses are available online with live instructor-led sessions. We also have hybrid options at select locations. Recordings of all sessions are available for review.

What kind of certification will I receive?

Upon successful completion, you'll receive a verified certificate from Tech Academy. Many of our courses also prepare you for industry-recognized certifications like AWS, Cisco, or CompTIA.

Do you offer job placement assistance?

Yes! We have a dedicated career services team that offers resume reviews, interview preparation, and connects students with our network of hiring partners. Many courses include a capstone project for your portfolio.
